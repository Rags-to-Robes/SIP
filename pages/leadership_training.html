Training Flowchart

This flowchart outlines the steps for leaders helping those with poverty. Click on each step to learn more.

1. Identify support indicators
Food insecurity, clothing, housing needs, medical problems, addiction, withdrawing from community.
2. Address urgent needs
Food, clothing, shelter, medical care, addiction help, community connection.
3. Assess status
Determine if they are poor or needy to guide next steps.
▼
Assign mentor
Establish a relationship and/or assign a mentor.
Shift their mindset
Motivate using spiritual resources to encourage a belief in themselves and a higher purpose.
Address challenges
Remove impediments such as addiction, health, or legal challenges.
Develop skills
Help build skills and assets: education, job skills, financial management.
▼
Support recovery
Provide support until they are back on their feet.
