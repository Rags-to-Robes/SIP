Training Flowchart

This flowchart outlines the steps for leaders helping those with poverty, it is designed to empower individuals with a belief in a higher power, as well as themselves, so that lasting change can and will happen.

Step 1: Build Trust

Listen to community members and establish a foundation of respect.

Step 2: Assess Needs

Work with partners to identify the most urgent areas for support.

Step 3: Provide Training

Offer resources and guidance tailored to each individual's situation.

Step 4: Empower Community

Celebrate successes and encourage ongoing leadership development.
