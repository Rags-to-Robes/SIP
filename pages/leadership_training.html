Training Flowchart

This flowchart outlines the steps for leaders helping those with poverty. Hover over each step to learn more.

1. Identify people
Some indicators of those who need support could include: food insecurity, clothing, housing needs, medical problems, addiction, or withdrawal from the community.
2. Address urgent needs
Help provide food, clothing, shelter, medical care, addiction help, community connection, and anything else they need.
3. Assess poverty status
Determine if they are poor (never been independent, lacks skills/resources) or needy (temporarily struggling despite past independence) to guide next steps.
