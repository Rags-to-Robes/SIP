Mindset Matters: A New Way to Talk About Poverty

Too often, the solution to poverty is framed as “more”—more resources, more money, more programs. But what if the missing piece is language and alignment?

Rags to Robes believes the real solution lies in a shared mindset. When spiritual communities align their language, purpose, and belief in human dignity, they become unstoppable agents of change.
